PEA

PEA

Operation:
SP - 4 -->; SP; <ea> -->; (SP)

Assembler Syntax:
PEA <ea>

Attributes: Size = (Long)

Description: Computes the effective address and pushes it onto
the stack. The effective address is a long-word address.

Condition Codes:
Not affected.

Example:

A1= $22222222
D0= $12345678
PEA (5,A1,D0)

On the stack the value: $3456789F
will be pushed.
D0 and A1 are unchanged.
